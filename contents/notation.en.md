## Normal Moves
- **Pawn moves**: Only the square is written (e4 → Pawn moves to e4).
- **Piece moves**: Use initials for pieces (Nf3 → Knight to f3).
- **Captures (`x`)**: Bxe5
- **Check (`+`)**: Qh5+
- **Checkmate (`#`)**: Qg7#

&nbsp;

## Special Moves
### **1. Castling**
- O-O → Kingside
- O-O-O → Queenside

### **2. Pawn Promotion**
- e8=Q → Pawn promotes to a queen.  

### **3. En Passant**
- exd6 e.p. → Pawn on **e5** captures **d5 en passant**.
